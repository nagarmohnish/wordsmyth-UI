Why It Matters

Wordsmyth was built in 1998 with one belief: no child should be denied access to a great dictionary because of money.

No venture capital. No data harvesting. No paywalls. Just a small team keeping quality education free for every child.

Keeping it free gets harder every year. Server costs rise, ad revenue declines. We refuse to compromise — so we're asking you.
